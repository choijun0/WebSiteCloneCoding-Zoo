  • zoo

    Zoo is a Paris-based graphic design studio founded in 2013 by Baptiste de l’Espinay, Julie Galand and Valentine Thébaut. We specialize in print design (publishing, layout, identity, etc.), web design (website, interface, motion design, etc.) and signage (sign, museography, etc.). We believe in strength of plain ideas and in necessity of a clear language. Each project is an adventure with new issues, driven by the care of functionality and aesthetic. Feel free to contact us, it will be a pleasure to meet you.
